## What it is

A personal study tool for preparing for the **CIH (Certified Industrial Hygienist)** exam — one of the more demanding professional credentials in occupational health.

Rather than rely on scattered PDFs and flashcard apps, I built a single self-contained tool around a structured question bank:

- A **growing bank of exam-style questions**, each with the correct answer and a worked explanation of *why* — not just the letter.
- **Topic classification** so practice can target weak areas (toxicology, ventilation, sampling and analysis, calculations, and so on).
- **Progress tracking** to see coverage and review history over time.
- A pipeline that ingests new question files, classifies them, drafts answers and explanations, and inserts them into the bank — so the tool grows as I study.

## Why build it

The act of building the tool is itself part of studying: structuring questions by topic, writing the explanations, and deciding what "knowing it" looks like forces a deeper engagement with the material than passive review. It's a small example of the lab's broader theme — turning a body of knowledge into something navigable, answerable, and practiced.

*A study aid built for my own preparation.*
